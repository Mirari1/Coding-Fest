ABOUT


Silvia Pelissero is an Italian artist better known as Agnes Cecile residing and working in Rome, Italy. Using simple images coupled with abstract color and detail, Agnes Cecile creates rich and emotional human portraits. Her speed painting video series on YouTube has gained the attention of millions of viewers thus building a massive fan base online.
The artist specializes in fine art giclee prints produced on fine art paper or canvas with archival, pigment based inks. The distinguishing features of her works are extremely accurate color, exceptional image quality, and unprecedented archival permanence.

Each print is individual and custom made. Please, visit Shop page to discover the available sizes and formats.
